Journal Submission

Title: Comparative Evaluations of Selected Tracking-by-Detection Approaches. Authors: A. A. Mekonnen and F. Lerasle

Abstract: In this work, we present a comparative evaluation of various multi-person tracking-by-detection approaches on public datasets. The work investigates popular sequential Monte Carlo and template ensemble based trackers coupled with relevant visual people detectors with emphasis on exhibited performance variation depending on tracker-detector choices. Our experimental results show that tracking is sensitive to the detector choice and should be done after careful evaluation. Even a 1% difference in detector recall could lead to a 10% drop in tracking accuracy.
